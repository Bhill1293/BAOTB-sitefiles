Sleepy Rose Drops Ethereal:Hell Edition

Gaining momentum as a 2020 XXL Freshman candidate, and a artist on Atlantics imprint TRU, helmed by 2 Chainz. 21 Year old Georgia native Sleepy Rose has been steadily attracting more fans and listeners with his music. His latest offering, split into two parts (A heaven and hell edition) features plenty of bangers to boot. The hell edition features the track 'Use It' produced by B.A. On The Beat.
